Run your rules testing for WFTDA Roller Derby.

How does it work?

  1. Start a new group and give it a name and your email.
  2. We email you two links: one for people to take the test, one for you (or others) to see results.
  3. Share the first link with anyone that should take the test.
  4. Each test taker enters their name and runs through all the questions.
  5. Test takers see their result and any wrong answers immediately after completing.
  6. Using the other link, you can see the scores of everyone who finished the test.

When you make your group, we randomly pick 40 questions out of our total set of ~100. Everyone in one group gets the same questions, but in a different order with a different answer order.

Who wrote the current questions?
All current questions were written by Goose Bumps of Parliament of Pain.
How should I use this in my league?
Rules testing is no longer required by WFTDA, so it's up to you. Some leagues require a minimum score to graduate as a rookie, some use it as an input for training, or for people to check their own understanding. Be mindful that some people have trouble with written questions but are great on track, and vice versa.
